(0) - 27 : Increase card limit from 21 to 27
(1) - 17 : Decrease card limit from 21 to 17
(2) - Refresh : Discards hand, draws 2 random cards and ends turn
(3) - Mistake : Discards the last drawn card
(4) - 7-Draw : Draws a 7 and ends turn
(5) - 3-Loss : Draws a -3 
(6) - Swap : Choose to swap a card with the dealer
(7) - Stop : Choose to swap a card with another random card
(8) - Hand-Swap : Choose to swap your entire hand with the dealer
(9) - Wild-Card : Draw a card representing any value and ends your turn
